Cucumber Salad Recipe

Description

This is the greatest crusty pasta you'll ever see. I swear by it.

Ingredients

  • 400g penne or macaroni
  • 1 red onion, roughly chopped
  • 250g head broccoli, stalks chopped and florets halved
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 1 tsp French or German mustard
  • 200g pot half fat crème fraîche
  • 100g grated mature gruyère or cheddar

Steps

  1. Halve the cucumber lengthways, then scoop out the seeds and slice. Mix with mustard seeds, vinegar and sugar.
