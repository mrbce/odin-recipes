Cucumber Salad Recipe

Description

This is the greatest and easiest salad ever. Seriously it's one step long.

Ingredients

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 tbsp black mustard
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp caster sugar

Steps

  1. Halve the cucumber lengthways, then scoop out the seeds and slice. Mix with mustard seeds, vinegar and sugar.
