Easy Pancakes Recipe

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make delicious pancakes in a few simple steps.
Follow along and you'll be sure to taste the greatest pancakes ever (I promise..)

Ingredients

  • 100g plain flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tbsp sunflower or vegetable oil
  • lemon wedges to serve
  • caster sugar to serve
